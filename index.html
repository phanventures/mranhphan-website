The Story

You go further when you're not going at it alone.

My parents came to America from Vietnam with no English, no safety net, and no guarantee it would work out. They were chasing the American dream from scratch, and they made it happen. That work ethic was in the air I grew up breathing. You don't wait for permission. You go after what you want. And when you fall down — and you will — you pick yourself back up and keep moving.

What I learned later is that ambition alone isn't enough. Most people with big dreams get stopped by fear. What changes everything is being around the right people: people who are fearless, who take risks, who show you by example that it's actually possible. People who are genuinely cheering for you. That kind of community doesn't just make the journey less lonely. It makes you believe it can happen.

I want everyone in my communities to feel like they belong and have a real place at the table. Like their ideas are worth something. Like there is always space for them, regardless of who does or doesn't approve. At the end of the day, I want people to feel that their life is worth living. That's the core of why I build communities: to help people find their people.

The Phan Club

A community for queer entrepreneurs — and the people who show up for them.

The Phan Club is a space for queer entrepreneurs and their allies to share wins, support each other, and build real connections. This isn't just an online community — we're working toward in-person retreats, visits, and shared experiences so we can grow and learn together in ways that actually stick.

Everything here is built around uplifting each other. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, navigating a hard decision, or just looking for people who get it — this is the place.

We're queer-led and values-forward. The majority of us are queer, but if you're a genuine ally who shows up for this community, you belong here too.

Right now, we're in founding member mode — building the first core group of people who will shape what this becomes. Founding members get grandfathered into a special rate and locked in for the long haul. We want people who are here to stay.
